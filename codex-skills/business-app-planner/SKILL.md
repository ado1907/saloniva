# Business App Planner

Use this skill to turn app ideas into sellable business products.

## Planning Principles

- Start from a real business pain, not a feature list.
- Prefer products with repeated use, clear money value, and simple sales messaging.
- Define the buyer, daily user, and first demo moment.
- Keep the MVP small enough to finish, but strong enough to sell.
- Plan Web, Android, and iOS only when the workflow benefits from all three.

## Prioritization

Rank features by:

1. Revenue impact
2. Daily usage
3. Demo value
4. Implementation effort
5. Store and legal readiness

## Output Shape

- Summarize the product in one sentence.
- List the core modules in build order.
- Define what makes the app different from generic competitors.
- Include pricing ideas when useful.
- Identify risks: data privacy, payments, platform rules, operational complexity, or support load.

## Saloniva Guidance

- Emphasize salon revenue, missed appointments, customer retention, package/session tracking, payments, and WhatsApp follow-up.
- Make the product easy to demonstrate to a salon owner in under five minutes.
- Avoid adding broad enterprise features before the core salon workflow feels excellent.
