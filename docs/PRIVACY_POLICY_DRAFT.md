# Saloniva Privacy Policy Draft

Last updated: 20 May 2026

This document is a draft and must be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before public release.

## 1. Who we are
Saloniva is a salon management application for appointment, customer, package, session and payment tracking.

## 2. Data we may collect
- Account data: name, email address and role
- Salon data: salon name, plan, branch and staff information
- Customer data entered by the salon: customer name, phone number, notes, appointments and package status
- Operational data: appointment time, service name, staff name, payment status and audit records
- Technical data: device type, app version, error logs and usage diagnostics when enabled

## 3. Why we use data
- To create and manage salon accounts
- To show appointments, customers, services, packages and payments
- To protect salon data through authentication and access rules
- To improve app reliability and user experience
- To provide support and account-related communication

## 4. Data separation
Saloniva is designed so each salon can access only its own data. The Supabase backend uses Row Level Security policies as the foundation for tenant separation.

## 5. Data sharing
We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers such as Supabase, hosting providers and payment providers when configured for production.

## 6. Account deletion
Users must be able to request account deletion from the app or from a published web link. A production deployment should include a public account deletion page and a support email.

## 7. Data retention
Salon data should be retained only as long as needed for the service, legal obligations and legitimate business purposes. Production retention rules must be finalized before public launch.

## 8. Security
Saloniva uses authentication, role-based access planning and database-level security policies. Production deployments should also enable backups, monitoring, rate limits and secret rotation.

## 9. Contact
Support email: support@saloniva.app

## 10. Legal review
This policy is a product draft. It is not legal advice and must be reviewed before App Store or Google Play submission.
